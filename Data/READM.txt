Due to government controls, the data  used to replicate our study (AGSS_minimal_secure.zip, EthioSIS_minimal_secure.zip, Other_minimal_secure.zip) files are 256-bit encrypted. To access the encryption keys please email signed permissions from the contact persons above to the authors.


To access the data from EthioSIS you must contact Kiflu Gudeta, Soil Resource Information and Mapping Directorate, Ministry of Agriculture, Ethiopia (email: gkiflu@gmail.com). AgSS data can be accessed by contacting the Data Administrator of Central Statistical Agency, Ethiopia (email: data@csa.gov.et).
